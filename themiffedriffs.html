Hey Riffters! Thank you so so much for all your support last night at the Hustler - the energy was absolutely through the roof - it won't be a night any of us will forget any time soon.

We'll be going on a much deserved break for the next few weeks, but keep your ear close to the ground, you never know when we might sprout up again! In the meantime, take care of yourself (by listening to our music, of course) and we'll see you all again soon!

From The Emperor

Hey, guys.

Just wanted to say thank you for all the kind words and support I've been getting from you all in regard to Death. Their passing was incredibly sudden and a terrible shock to me and the rest of the band.

As many of you probably know, Death and I met at one of our very first gigs, and told us from the outset that we had something really amazing going, to stick with it, and let it grow. Without those words of encouragement this band would not be where it is today.

Thank you all again for your kind words of support and love, I hope to get back playing with the band as soon as I'm up to it. Please wait for me until then.

- E

About Us

Heya! We're an indie alternative band based in Melbourne, Australia, performing at all your favourite local joints.

We make music about just about anything - from the capitalistic destruction of the planet to the last bits of honey stuck at the bottom of a pot.

Initially formed during our first years at university at an attempt to make each other look cooler than we really were, we went from shitty pub to shitty pub until for some reason more and more of you started asking when our next gig was.

Thanks to all of your brilliant support, we were able to release our first proper album! Check it out here!
