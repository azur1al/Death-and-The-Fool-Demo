# Demo for Death and The Fool
A demo for my Death and The Fool project - 2,000 words of content.

<p>This Twine is a proof of concept/demo version of the skeleton for the end portion of my piece. When the piece is complete, the only aspect that will remain the same is the text.</p>
<p>The final version will be a never-ending, constantly-looping interrogation of the characters, accompanied by a slightly unsettling shifting soundscape, portraits of the suspects, and pieces of evidence you can view.</p>
<p>The CSS, soundscape, and images used are royalty-free placeholders.</p>
